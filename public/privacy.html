Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What We Collect

AChat stores the minimum data needed to operate chat rooms and device sessions, including account identifiers, room membership, and encrypted message payloads.

Message Content

Message content is end-to-end encrypted on client devices. The server stores encrypted payloads and related delivery metadata required for synchronization.

Account Hash Responsibility

Your account hex is your credential. If you lose it, access recovery is not guaranteed. Keep it private and stored securely.

Security

We apply technical controls to protect the service, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Users should also follow strong device and credential hygiene.
